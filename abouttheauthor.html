Who is the author of Scruff

"WRITER & CREATOR Adhim DeVeaux, affectionately known as “Brother DeVeaux”, is an educator, activist, father and HBCU Hampton University Alum. Growing up in Mount Vernon New York, he was fascinated with role playing games, character creation and comic books. This lifelong passion and enthusiasm for the genre lays the foundation for an intriguing story. In The Unforgiving Scruff, Adhim explores the intersections of trauma, race, masculinity, and mental health to create a new perspective of the urban vigilante. The Unforgiving Scruff is Adhim DeVeaux’s first comic book. "
